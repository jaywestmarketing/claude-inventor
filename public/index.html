Frequently Asked Questions

What is AutomateHub?

AutomateHub is a collection of free, browser-based automation tools designed for business professionals. Each tool helps automate repetitive tasks like invoice generation, meeting scheduling, employee onboarding, and lead tracking — saving you hours every week.

Who are these automation tools for?

Our tools are built for HR managers, office managers, small business owners, CEOs, desk clerks, marketing teams, sales teams, and anyone in an office role who wants to automate repetitive tasks and boost productivity.

Are these tools really free?

Yes. All tools on AutomateHub are free to use directly in your browser. No downloads, no credit card required. Premium features may be available in the future for power users.

Do I need to install anything?

No. Every tool runs entirely in your web browser. There's nothing to download or install. Works on any device with a modern browser — desktop, tablet, or mobile.

How do I get early access to new tools?

Join our waitlist on any tool page. You'll be the first to know when new tools launch and get priority access to configure and use them before the public release.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. Most tools process data entirely in your browser — nothing is sent to our servers. For tools that require server processing, we use industry-standard encryption and never sell your data.

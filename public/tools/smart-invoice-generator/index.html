Invoice Generator FAQ

How do I create a free invoice online?

Simply open AutomateHub's Smart Invoice Generator, enter your business details and client information, add line items with descriptions and rates, and click "Download PDF." No account or signup needed — your invoice is generated entirely in your browser.

Is this invoice generator really free?

Yes, 100% free. There are no hidden fees, no watermarks on your invoices, and no limit on how many invoices you can create. The tool runs entirely in your browser.

Is my data safe with this invoice tool?

Your data never leaves your browser. We don't store any invoice data on our servers. Everything is processed locally on your device, making this one of the most privacy-friendly invoicing tools available.

Can I save invoice templates for reuse?

Yes. Click "Save Template" to store your invoice settings in your browser's local storage. You can reload them anytime without creating an account.

What makes this better than Invoice Ninja or Wave?

Unlike Invoice Ninja (which requires a complex setup) or Wave (which forces account creation), AutomateHub's invoice generator works instantly with zero setup. It's also 100% browser-based, meaning your financial data stays on your device.

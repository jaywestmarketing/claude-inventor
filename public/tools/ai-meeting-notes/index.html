Meeting Notes Summarizer FAQ

What is an AI meeting notes summarizer?

An AI meeting notes summarizer is a tool that uses artificial intelligence to analyze meeting transcripts or raw notes and generate structured summaries. It automatically extracts key decisions, action items with assignees, deadlines, and follow-up tasks — saving you 30-60 minutes of manual note organization per meeting.

Does this tool join my meetings like Otter.ai or Fireflies?

No. Unlike Otter.ai, Fireflies.ai, or Read.ai, AutomateHub's meeting notes tool never joins your meetings. There is no bot, no recording, and no live transcription. You paste your existing notes or transcript after the meeting, and the AI summarizes them. This means zero privacy concerns and no awkward bot participants in your calls.

Is my meeting data safe?

Yes. The demo version processes your notes entirely in your browser using pattern matching — no data is sent to any server. The full version will use AI processing with clear disclosure, but the demo is 100% local and private.

How is this different from Otter.ai, Fireflies, or Fathom?

The biggest differences are privacy and simplicity. Otter.ai and Fireflies require bots that join your meetings, record audio, and auto-email transcripts to participants — often without consent. Fathom shows a visible "Notetaker" participant. Read.ai was banned by universities for "virus-like" behavior. AutomateHub is free, requires no signup, has no bot, and processes everything in your browser.

What meeting types does this tool support?

The tool supports multiple meeting templates: daily standups, 1-on-1 meetings, board meetings, sales calls, client meetings, interviews, sprint reviews, and general meetings. Each template optimizes the AI summary format for that meeting type.

Can I export my meeting summaries?

Yes. Export summaries as PDF (for sharing), Markdown (for docs and wikis), or copy to clipboard as plain text. You can also save summaries to your browser's local storage for later reference.
